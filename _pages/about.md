I am a PhD candidate at the School of Computer Science, Northwestern Polytechnical University. My research focuses on Multimodal Fusion, including cross-modal representation learning, robust fusion under missing modalities, and lightweight deployment of multimodal large models. I am passionate about open source and knowledge sharing, with a track record of high-impact publications and industry‑collaboration projects. My goal is to advance multimodal intelligence in real‑world applications through reproducible research and tools.
